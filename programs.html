Neural

Program Description

(N-P2) Augen's Program 2 (Neural) will introduce the groundbreaking Brain-Computer Interface A¹ Neuro, underscoring its commitment to advanced wearable technology. This wearable device will integrate IoT Connectivity, feature an AI-powered Autonomous Personal Agent, offer Advanced Health Functionalities, and provide Mind Power Capabilities, ensuring ultra-immersive life experiences never seen before.

The company will develop a wearable, non-invasive, wireless, and portable Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) that extends beyond medical use, aiming for integration into daily life with safer and more accessible applications. It will tackle the challenges of matching the spatial resolution of non-invasive BCIs to that of implants but remains optimistic in research and development efforts to overcome these hurdles. It envisions a future where BCIs will enable direct brain-computer communications, enhancing cognitive abilities and redefining human potential for everyone.

(BCI) With A¹ Neuro, the first Augen Invisible Neural Computing will be born.

In the realm of wearable technology, Augen's Program 2 takes a visionary leap forward with the development of the A¹ Neuro a Wearable Brain-Computer Interface. A¹ Neuro is a state-of-the-art wearable device that integrates IoT Connectivity, features an Autonomous AI-powered Personal Agent, offers advanced Health Functions, and provides Mind Power Capabilities.
