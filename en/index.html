About MIMCO Capital

At MIMCO Capital, we value meaningful real estate.
Each of our projects represents a new opportunity to build for the future.

In a constantly changing world, with evolving habits and lifestyles, MIMCO Capital creates value by ensuring that real estate remains in line with market expectations and user needs.

We adapt real estate to the current times, in particular through the use of technology and in compliance with the latest energy standards, to enable it to stand the test of time, while minimizing its impact on the environment.

Discover our services at MIMCO

  • Fund Raising

  • Investment Advisory

  • Financial Engineering

  • Real Estate Asset Management

  • Project Development

/01

Fund Raising

MIMCO connects professional investors wishing to invest in real estate and sharing the same objectives. At the heart of the European Union, we have established solid, quality relationships with major investors and lenders. These contacts constitute the cornerstone of our collective investment approach, creating value, serving asset revaluation projects.

/02

Investment Advisory

Through its consulting activity, MIMCO supports the implementation of investment vehicles adapted to the strategy defined with clients and investment partners. We integrate into the very heart of our team a diversity of expertise and a solid strategic knowledge base allowing us to consider the implementation of solutions adapted to the specificities of each project.

/03

Financial Engineering

MIMCO positions itself as a key player in the field of financial engineering, offering personalized services and innovative solutions to help its clients maximize their financial efficiency, manage risks and achieve their long-term business objectives. This expertise in financial engineering helps to strengthen the stability and growth of the companies with which MIMCO works.

/04

Real Estate Asset Management

MIMCO develops locally expert real estate management. Our teams are geographically close to the assets under management and share the culture of the markets in which we operate. We develop, through this active presence, expertise and a network allowing us to constantly optimize the performance of our investments.

/05

Project Development

Our teams support and implement investment projects for the revaluation and development of quality properties. We carry out our mission through a wide spectrum of services, ranging from the purchase of virgin plots, to the sale and restructuring of existing properties.
